  • Refrigerated Hauling

    Refrigerated Hauling
    Our Refrigerated Transport meets unique needs of moving temp-sensitive goods, ensuring pristine arrival. Maintaining precise temps, whether edibles, meds, or perishables, is paramount.

    Modern refrigerated trailers, trucks with advanced cooling and real-time temp monitoring maintain consistency. Drivers are trained to handle these shipments, adhering to strict protocols.

    Quality and safety are paramount. Regular maintenance and servicing of refrigeration units ensure peak performance. We follow industry regulations for food safety and pharma transport.

  • Flatbed Hauling

    U.Z. Freight INC understands that some shipments require extra space and flexibility. Our Flatbed Hauling service is perfect for oversized, overweight, or irregular cargo. Equipped with specialized flatbed trailers and experienced drivers, we excel at moving even the toughest loads.

    Our flatbed trailers come in various sizes, perfect for diverse cargo dimensions. From machinery to building materials, we've got the equipment and expertise.
